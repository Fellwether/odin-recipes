Grandma Vincenza Forte's Chicken Meatballs

Simple, delicious chicken meatballs from the Forte side of the family

    Ingredients:
  • cut up chunks of raw chicken
  • bread crumbs
  • cut up chunks of raw chicken
  • onion powder
  • salt
  • garlic powder
  • 1-2 eggs (depending on amount of other ingredients
    Steps:
  1. Mix together all ingredients in bowl.
  2. form balls based on size preference
  3. fry in oil
