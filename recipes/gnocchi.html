Gnocchi

A classic homemade pasta. Pronounce "nYaw-kee", despite what restaurants tell you.

    Ingredients:
  • 1 egg
  • 1 medium potato, mashed OR 1/2 lb ricotta
  • flour
    Steps:
  1. mix ricotta or potato plus egg
  2. add enough flour to make non-sticky dough
  3. roll into ropes
  4. cut into small tootsie-roll shapes
  5. Roll with your thumb (to curl)
  6. cook in boiling, salted water for about 20 mins
